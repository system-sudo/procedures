### **C**loud **S**ecurity **A**lliance (CSA)
It is the world's leading not-for-profit organization committed to awareness, implementation, and credentialing of AI, cloud, and Zero Trust.  
with a mission to “promote the use of best practices for providing security assurance within Cloud Computing,  
and to provide education on the uses of Cloud Computing to help secure all other forms of computing.”
The CSA Cloud Controls Matrix (CCM) is a cybersecurity control framework for cloud computing.
It is composed of 197 control objectives that are structured in 17 domains covering all key aspects of cloud technology.
