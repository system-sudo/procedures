## HIPAA
HIPAA is the acronym for the **H**ealth **I**nsurance **P**ortability and **A**ccountability **A**ct  
that sets national standards for protecting sensitive patient health information, ensuring health coverage portability, and standardizing electronic healthcare transactions to improve efficiency and security  
It gives patients rights over their data and requires healthcare providers and businesses to safeguard **P**rotected **H**ealth **I**nformation (PHI).  

#### Key Aspects of HIPAA:

* Privacy Rule: Protects PHI, allowing its use and disclosure only under specific conditions.
* Security Rule: Sets national standards for securing electronic PHI (ePHI).
* Portability: Ensures workers can maintain health insurance when changing jobs.
* Administrative Simplification: Mandates electronic transaction standards and identifiers for healthcare entities.
